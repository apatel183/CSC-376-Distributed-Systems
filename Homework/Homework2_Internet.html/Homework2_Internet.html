Homework 2: How the Internet Works

Answer the following questions:

  1. What term describes a collection of connected networks?
  2. What model do we use to describe the layers of software and hardware involved in networking?
  3. What are the two predominant transport protocols used for the Internet?

  5. What is CIDR? What is is used for?
  6. What system is used to map domain names to IP addresses?
  7. What is a TLD? What is DePaul's TLD?
  8. The original mapping of a domain name to an IP address is supplied to the DNS system by what server?
  9. Packets are routed through the Internet by what entities?
  10. What tool reveals the route packets travel from your host to a destination host?
  11. Which version of IP is used most commonly today?
  12. What is the size of an IPv4 address?
  13. What two addresses are contained in the header of an IP packet?
  14. What is the payload of an IP packet?
  15. TCP connections are formed using what procedure?
  16. Name 3 characteristics of TCP that are not shared with UDP.
  17. TCP is known as a streaming protocol-- why?
  18. What are the first two fields in the TCP header?
  19. Give two reasons why UDP is used.
  20. What transport protocol do DNS and DHCP use?
